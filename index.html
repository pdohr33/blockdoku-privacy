BlockDoku Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-02

Summary

BlockDoku is an offline puzzle game. The game stores progress and settings on your device. The app shows ads using Google AdMob.

Data we collect

  • Local game data: scores, streaks, daily history, and settings are stored on your device only.
  • Advertising data: the app uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and basic diagnostics to serve and measure ads.

How we use data

  • To save your game progress and settings locally.
  • To display and measure ads (via AdMob).

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Advertising data may be processed by Google as part of AdMob ad serving and measurement.

Children

BlockDoku is not directed to children under 13.
